  IGI 2

 I.G.I 2 is one of very beautiful shooting games. This game belongs from the series of IGI and it is the second installment of this series. It is very famous game and people all over the world love this game.
This game is developed by Innerloop Studios and published by Codemasters. It was released on March 3,2003.

he main character of the IGI 2 is Dived Jones who is the agent of secrete British institute. The story of the game is that the Russian Mafia stolen
some secrete EMP chips. So now Phillip White who is the official of this institute gave task to the player to get these chips back in this mission Phillip White will command the player.
