 Earm TO DIE 2

 
Earn to Die 2 is developed by Toffee Games and is published under the banner of Not
This game was released on 5th May, 2016. In this 2D side scrolling racing game you need to travel through zombie apocalypse. Where you will face zombies in huge numbers.
You need to upgrade your vehicle in such a way that they crush all the zombies that come your way. You can also download ATV Quadro Racing.

Earn to Die 2 has got hours of extra gameplay as well as some new vehicles.
It has also got some new levels plus an unlockable Mission Mode. Then there is a Story Mode which is full of action and is 10 times longer than the online version.
The vehicles are of different variety which includes a police car, a sports car and an ice cream van. You need to arm them with different weapons in order to take zombies down.
It features fantastic ragdoll physics where you can smash the zombies and then see them flying all around. The graphics are impressive and you will surely love this game. You can also download Police Super Cars Racing.
