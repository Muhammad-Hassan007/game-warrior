 FREEDOM FIGHTER






  "Freedom Fighters" is a third-person shooter game developed by IO Interactive and published by Electronic Arts.
    It was released for various platforms, including PC, in 2003. The game is set in an alternate history where the Soviet Union has invaded and occupied New York City, and players take on the role of a plumber turned resistance fighter.
